Odoo 11.0 : Hospital Management

How to use

Summary:

Hospital Management System is generic system which can be helpful for any
Hospital and medical facilities which comprise of below features:
- Patients
- Appointments
- Prescriptions
- Lab Test
and many more

Patients

You can enter all the details of patients.All other tabs will show details about patients like , medicaments, lab test details, etc...


Patients Card Report

Reports for Patients Card can be generated.


Patients Medication History Report

Reports for Patients Medication History can be generated.


Appointments

From this tab user can create an appointment for a patient with tiny details like physician , consultation service etc..


Here we have feature showing calendar to show appointments on particular dates.


Prescriptions

Prescription is feature where after diagnosis of the patient , you can enter the details of prescripted medicaments, its dosage and other details.


Prescription Report

Reports for Prescription can be generated.


Patient Hospitalization

This form will help administrative department to save the details of patient about his/her hospitalization. Patients bed details, hospitalization date & expected discharge date, and other details can be saved here.


Once "confirmed registration" registration will be in confirmed stage. From this stage it can be cancelled.


If patient gets admitted to hospital then you will see discharge patient button.


Invoice

Prescription Invoice.


Appointments Invoice.
